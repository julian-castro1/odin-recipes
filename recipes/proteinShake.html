Peanut Butter Protein Shake

Ingredients:

  • peanut butter
  • milk
  • ice
  • protein powder
  • honey
  • banana

Steps:

  1. load the ice into the blender
  2. load the milk into the blender
  3. load the protein powder into the blender
  4. load the peanut butter into the blender
  5. load the honey into the blender
  6. load the banana into the blender
    7. blend until smooth
  7. enjoy!
