High Protein Lemon Pepper Chicken Meal

Ingredients:

  • chicken breast
  • lemon pepper seasoning
  • salt
  • pepper
  • white rice
  • tomato

Steps:

  1. season chicken with lemon pepper, salt, and pepper
  2. set the rice to cook in a rice cooker
  3. cook the chicken on medium heat
  4. slice the tomato into thin slices
  5. when everything is ready, place the rice, then the chicken on top. Line the tomato slices along the side.
  6. Enjoy!
